It is a thrilling event where participants
showcase their Robotic Engineering skills in a fast-paced Competition.
Participants should prepare a "Line-follower Robot" using Arduino.
Robots will navigate through challenging tracks,
demonstrating speed, agility,
and precision.

GUIDELINES

1.Robot should be an "Aurdino based Line-follower Robot".
2.The robot should complete the Race Track.
3.Evaluation is based on time and efficiency.
